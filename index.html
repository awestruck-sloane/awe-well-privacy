Awe Well

One small wonder, every day

Awe isn't only for grand canyons. The everyday kind, a song, a stranger's kindness, light through a window, adds up faster than the rare big experiences. Awe Well is a quiet daily practice for noticing it.

The whole practice, in a minute a day

Notice something, write down what you noticed, get on with your day.
No streaks, no guilt, no scores.

One invitation a day

A small prompt written to fit your sources of wonder. Turn on location tailoring and it can fit the weather and the hour too. When conditions outside aren't safe, Awe Well keeps you comfortably indoors.

Listen moments

Some days the wonder arrives as sound: a humpback whale singing, lightning at Jupiter caught by a passing spacecraft, dawn breaking over Yellowstone.

Somewhere new

Arrive somewhere you haven't been and Awe Well can point out one nearby thing worth a look, led by a true story about it.

A journal of what you noticed

Reflect by typing or speaking. Your voice becomes editable text on your device, and no audio is ever kept. Everything you noticed, searchable, newest first.

A quiet app, on purpose

A few honest screens. Nothing fighting for your attention.

Private by design

Your reflections belong to you. They sync to your account so they survive a new phone, and they are never sold, shared, or used to train AI. Location is optional, approximate, and never stored. Delete your account and everything goes with it, right from Settings.
