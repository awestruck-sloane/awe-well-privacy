Common questions

  • How do I change or turn off notifications? Open the app, tap the gear on the Today screen, and adjust the daily reminder or the "Somewhere new" nudges. You can also manage them in iPhone Settings under Notifications.
  • How do I control location access? Both location features are off by default and opt-in. Turn them on or off any time in the app's Settings, or in iPhone Settings under Privacy & Security, then Location Services.
  • How do I delete my account? In the app: gear icon, then "Delete account" at the bottom. This permanently removes your account and all your reflections. If you can't access the app, email us and we'll do it for you.
  • Where is my data stored? See our privacy policy for the full picture. The short version: your reflections are stored securely, your location never is, and nothing is sold or used for ads.
